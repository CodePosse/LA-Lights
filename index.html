A Lot More Than Lighting

Since its founding almost a century ago, the Los Angeles Bureau of Street Lighting has been on the forefront of innovation, technology, and design. As our lives become more and more digitally connected, the Bureau is continuously improving and enhancing LA’s street lights to make them provide even more benefits to our communities. See some of the ways that the Bureau has been expanding and exploring what is possible with our network of more than 200,000 street lights.
